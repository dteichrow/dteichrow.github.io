A ship was a moving disease ecosystem.

This exhibit follows disease as an ecology of ships: ports, provisions, crowded berths, labor, violence, vectors, quarantine, and the routes that made local exposures move.
How to use this exhibit

Choose a station group across the top, then move through the scene chips below. Guided Tour plays the exhibit without hiding the interface. Presentation Mode switches to the cinematic capture layout for a later YouTube cut.
