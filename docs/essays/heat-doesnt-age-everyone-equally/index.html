Card 01

Multiple choice

Which option best completes this essay point? "“_____” is a summary score extracted from health signals that correlate with future risk: either clinical-marker composites (the Taiwan study’s approach) or epigenetic clocks (built from..."

  1. Aresidual confounding
  2. Bclocks
  3. CBiological age
  4. Dage acceleration

Answer 01

Biological age

“Biological age” is a summary score extracted from health signals that correlate with future risk: either clinical-marker composites (the Taiwan study’s approach) or epigenetic clocks (built from DNA methylation).

Multiple choice

  1. Aclocks
  2. Bage acceleration
  3. CBiological age
  4. Dresidual confounding

Answer 02

age acceleration

Think of it as a noisy, unit-converted risk meter where higher biological age relative to your actual age, so-called “age acceleration”, generally tracks with worse outcomes in longitudinal studies.

Card 09

Short answer

What caveat does the essay make about What “biological age” actually is (and isn’t)?

Answer 09

It’s not per year, and it’s not meant to be stacked linearly for decades since people move, adapt, change jobs, buy AC, and the models just aren’t built to extrapolate across half a lifetime.
