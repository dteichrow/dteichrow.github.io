Multiple choice

  3. COld World
  4. DNorth America and Central America

Answer 04

Chilean and Argentine

Notably, Andes virus is endemic to Chilean and Argentine temperate and Patagonian forests and represents the only hantavirus known to transmit person-to-person, with pulmonary syndrome cases recorded since the mid-1970s.
