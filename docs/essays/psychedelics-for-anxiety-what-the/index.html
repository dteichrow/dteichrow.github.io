What point does the Substack essay make about phase multicenter randomized double-blind?

Answer 07

A phase 2b, multicenter, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial gave one supervised dose of either 25, 50, 100, or 200 µg to adults with moderate to severe GAD and followed them for 12 weeks.
